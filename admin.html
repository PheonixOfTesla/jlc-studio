Service

From intimate dinner arrangements to grand wedding installations, each bloom tells your unique story.

With years of experience leading floral departments for luxury events, we approach each arrangement as a work of art. Our designs blend classic elegance with contemporary flair, using seasonal blooms and unexpected elements to create unforgettable impressions.

Service

Complete venue transformations that create immersive experiences and unforgettable atmospheres.

We don't just decorate spaces—we transform them. From conceptual design through on-site installation, we handle every detail. Our background in fashion and visual design brings a unique perspective to event styling that elevates every celebration.

Service

Bespoke garments and alterations crafted with precision from our NYC fashion heritage.

With a background that includes New York Fashion Week runway shows and work with legendary designers, we bring couture-level craftsmanship to every piece. From wedding dress alterations to custom garments designed from scratch, each stitch reflects our commitment to perfection.
