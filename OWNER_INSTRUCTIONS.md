## What You Can Edit

When you log in, you'll see a sidebar with all editable sections:

### Website Content
1. **Hero Banner** - The main banner visitors see first
   - Badge text (small text above title)
   - Title lines 1 & 2
   - Subtitle
   - Description
   - Button text
   - Hero image URL

2. **About / Bio** - Your personal information
   - Your name
   - Professional title
   - Bio text
   - Years of experience
   - Events created
   - Profile photo URL

3. **Services & Pricing** - All your service offerings
   - Floral Design services
   - Event Decor services
   - Custom Tailoring services
   - Each service has: name, description, price range

4. **Packages** - Bundled service packages
   - Package title
   - Description
   - Price
   - Featured (highlighted) option
   - What's included

5. **Testimonials** - Client reviews
   - Client name
   - Event/Role
   - Quote
   - Initials (for avatar display)

6. **FAQ** - Frequently asked questions
   - Question
   - Answer

7. **Contact Info** - Business contact details
   - Business name
   - Tagline
   - Phone
   - Email
   - Address
   - Instagram URL
   - Pinterest URL

### Appearance
8. **Color Theme** - Website colors
   - 10 preset color themes to choose from
   - Custom colors with RGB control
   - Primary, accent, dark, and background colors

### Business
9. **Analytics** - View booking and revenue data
   - Links to Calendly dashboard
   - Links to Stripe dashboard

10. **Calendly & Stripe** - Integration status
    - Both show as "Connected"
    - Quick access to external dashboards

---

## How to Make Changes

1. **Log in** at https://jlcstudio.art/admin
2. **Click a section** in the sidebar
3. **Edit the fields** - current data is pre-filled
4. **Click "Save"** - changes deploy in ~30 seconds
5. **Refresh your website** to see changes
