Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings and large events, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. Smaller events can often be accommodated with 2-4 weeks notice, depending on availability.

Do you offer consultations?

Yes! We offer complimentary initial consultations to discuss your vision and determine if we're the right fit for your project.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Sarasota and serve the surrounding areas including Bradenton, Venice, Lakewood Ranch, and beyond. Travel fees may apply for distant locations.

Can I combine multiple services?

Absolutely! Our unique triple-service model means you can have coordinated florals, decor, and tailoring from one creative vision. We offer package pricing for combined services.
