Meet the Designer

JLynne Huffer

A multidisciplinary creative professional with over 15 years of experience spanning fashion design, event decor, and floral artistry.

From the runways of New York Fashion Week to luxury events in Denver and now Sarasota, JLynne brings a unique perspective that blends artistic vision with technical expertise. Having worked with celebrity clients at Mood Fabrics and assisted on multiple seasons of Project Runway, she understands how to create experiences that are both visually stunning and deeply personal.
