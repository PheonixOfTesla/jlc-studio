A Journey of Creativity

With over 15 years of experience spanning fashion design, event decor, textile development, floral design, and creative instruction, Jeremy Huffer brings a rare combination of artistic vision and technical mastery to every project.

Born in the United States and raised in Sweden, Jeremy's international background—including three years living in Israel—has shaped a worldview that values diverse perspectives and cultural richness. Fluent in English and Swedish, with conversational Hebrew and Spanish, he connects with clients from all walks of life.

Jeremy's professional journey began in the heart of New York's fashion district. At Elise Overland, he developed and directed seasonal collections, produced patterns and tech packs, and supported runway shows at New York Fashion Week. His time at the legendary Mood Fabrics saw him sourcing textiles for both NYC and LA locations while designing for celebrity clients including Sarah Jessica Parker, Katie Holmes, and Damon Dash.

As a creative designer at Catering by Design in Denver, Jeremy led the Floral Department, overseeing concept creation, production, labor management, and purchasing budgets for luxury events. This role crystallized his vision for JLC Studio—a place where florals, decor, and tailoring come together under one creative roof.
